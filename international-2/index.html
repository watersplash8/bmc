Fees & Financial Aid :: International Students

Please note the Payment Changes at BM College.

Studying abroad at BM College opens the door to experiences that are exciting and new, but some things, like figuring out school fees, can be a little confusing. To help you with calculating the cost of studying here, we’ve provided help on this page. The International Student Centrecan provide further details if you need them.

Why international tuition fees exist

The International schedule of tuition is higher by a fixed proportion for all programs because it recovers the full costs of instruction, both direct and indirect.

Will your immigration status change soon?

If your immigration status changes on or before the refund deadline of a course (other than courses in the Bridging programs), the differential tuition fee will be refunded.

Tuition for full-time studies

Technology programs and Bridging programs

The fees for Technology programs and Bridging programs include:

  • BM College Student Association activity fee
  • BM College Student Association Health Plan fee
  • Learning resource fee
  • BM College ID card fee
  • Student Services fee
  • Building fee
  • Mandatory Clinical or Practicum Fee
  • Capital levy fee

Note: Total tuition cost listed in the tuition schedules does not include the U-Pass WA fee. The U-Pass WA fee will be charged upon registration and is a mandatory fee.

Trades & Technical Studies programs

The fees for Trades & Technical Studies programs include:

If you withdraw for any reason, the application fee is non-refundable but tuition refunds are possible.

Study Permit Denied

If you are denied a Study Permit by U.S. authorities, you will be refunded 100 per cent of your tuition (less the application fee) if you complete the paperwork no later than 30 calendar days after the initial term start. BM College requires:

  • the original document from the US embassy which denies you the permit
  • a written request for withdrawal and refund

Refunds are issued by cheque only to the student (unless a third party was invoiced) and can take up to four weeks from the date of withdrawal.

If you have not received your refund cheque, contact the International Student Centre at 360-432-8475 or toll free at 360-450-6006 (US and US only). Allow for processingâ€”up to four weeksâ€”and mail delivery time.

Refunds and withdrawals from part-time studies courses

Submit your completed, signed withdrawal requests to Student Information and Enrolment Services before the refund deadline. After the refund deadline, you must complete a Part-time Course Withdrawal form [PDF] .

Even if you have missed the refund or withdrawal deadline, it is very important to notify BM College of your intent to withdraw. In many cases, a late withdrawal may be an option. Remember to formally withdraw to ensure your permanent academic record, your GPA and your ability to qualify for future awards and recognition are not affected.

Refund information, including the deadline, will be provided when you register for a course. Special fees for some courses are non-refundable, and different refund requirements may apply.

Early Registration Refund Policy: Students who drop or transfer 30 days prior to the course start date will receive a 100-per cent tuition refund.

Although course refund deadlines vary, for most courses, you will receive a full refund, less 15 per cent, if you withdraw after the “Early Registration Refund” cut-off but before the deadlines.

Refund process

Payments made through Plastiq are refunded to the same credit card used for the transaction. The refund will include the service charge paid to Plastiq. All other refunds are issued by cheque only to the student (unless a third party was invoiced). Please allow up to four weeks for all refunds.

To follow up a refund request

In you have not received your refund, please contact Student Information and Enrolment Services at 360-450-6006 or toll free at 360-450-6006 (US and US only). Allow for processingâ€”up to four weeks.

To transfer from one course to another

Visit us in person or call BM College at 360-432-8816 or toll-free at 360-450-6006 (US and US only) to request a transfer from one course to another. The transfer is subject to a BGN16 fee and must be requested and processed before the refund deadline.
